# Purpose of Website

I’m creating a website to introduce and invite people to a pixel-themed cultural fair celebrating/exploring pixel art, technology, music, and food through exhibitions, panels, and hands-on activities such as pixel game hackathons and DIY sprite and 8-bit music production workshops. 


# Intended Users

I would expect anyone interested in pixel art, music, and game development to attend this event. The fair is open to everyone to explore pixel culture while having fun. It is targeted at youth, adolescents, adults, and families. They can interact with my website through clicking buttons, scrolling, and rsvp forms
